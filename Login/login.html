‘Alone we can do so little, together we can do so much’. Common-interest communities are becoming a popular way of living. Such communities need the help of the members to get involved and ultimately take responsibility of their neighborhood. For smooth functioning of such Homeowner’s communities we propose a web-based full featured Homeowners Neighborhood management application – Smart Housing. Our application provides a comprehensive platform for the committee members to have a well-governed committee, means of effective communication, transparent accounting and professional management of community association.
